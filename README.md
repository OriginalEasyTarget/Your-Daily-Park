# Privacy Policy for Your Daily Park

**Last Updated:** February 26, 2026

Discover a different U.S. National Park every day!

## Overview

Your Daily Park ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**API Keys**: You may optionally provide your own API keys for the following services:
  - **National Park Service API**: For fetching official park images and information
  - **Unsplash API**: For additional high-quality nature photography

All API keys are stored locally on your device in secure keychain storage and are never transmitted to our servers. They are only used to make direct API requests to the respective services.

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Downloaded Images**: Park images are cached on your device for offline viewing and better performance.
- **User Preferences**: Display settings, font preferences, and other customization options are stored locally on your device.
- **Park History**: A record of previously viewed parks is stored locally on your device.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal information
- We do not track your location
- We do not use analytics or tracking tools
- We do not share any data with third parties
- We do not collect usage statistics
- We do not require account creation

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

### Flickr API
- **Purpose**: Fetch supplementary park images from public photo collections
- **Data Shared**: API requests include search terms (park names) and your API key (if provided)
- **Privacy Policy**: https://www.flickr.com/help/privacy
 
### National Park Service API
- **Purpose**: Fetch park images and information
- **Data Shared**: API requests include park names only
- **Privacy Policy**: https://www.nps.gov/aboutus/privacy.htm

### Unsplash API
- **Purpose**: Fetch additional high-quality nature and park photography
- **Data Shared**: API requests include search terms (park names) and your API key (if provided)
- **Privacy Policy**: https://unsplash.com/privacy

### Wikipedia API
- **Purpose**: Fetch park summaries and educational content
- **Data Shared**: API requests include park names only
- **Privacy Policy**: https://foundation.wikimedia.org/wiki/Privacy_policy

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:
- Images are cached in your device's storage
- Preferences are stored using iOS UserDefaults
- History is stored using SwiftData in your device's App Group container
- No data is transmitted to remote servers owned or operated by us

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. All data remains on your device.

## Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages (4+) and does not collect any personal information from children or adults.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete the App and all associated data at any time by uninstalling it
- Clear cached images through the App's Maintenance settings
- Reset preferences at any time

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised.
